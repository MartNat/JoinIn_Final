About Us

JoinIn is where university students turn their interests into connections! Whether you're passionate about sports, arts, coding, or anything in between, Joining helps you find and connect with like-minded peers.

With features tailored for student life, Joining lets you explore diverse communities, stay updated on events, and share your journey with others. It’s more than a network – it’s a place to grow, learn, and enjoy campus life to the fullest. Dive in, find your crowd, and start making memories today!

FAQ

What is JoinIn?

JoinIn is a platform for university students to connect over shared interests.

How can I join a community?

Simply sign up, explore the available communities, and join the ones that interest you.

Is there a fee to join?

Joining is free for students.

Can I create my own community?

Yes! You can start your own community and invite others to join.
